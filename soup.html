Mushroom and Lentil Soup

Here is how to make mushroom and lentil soup!

    Ingredients List
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1/3 cup cooked lentils
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 carrot
  • 1/3 cup mushrooms
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 2 tsp cayenne
    Steps to Cook
  1. pour broth into a pot and heat on medium heat
  2. add garlic powder, paprika, and cayenne
  3. put onions and carrots into pot
  4. leave to simmer in pot until carrots can be easily pierced with fork, roughly 10 - 15 minutes
  5. add mushrooms and lentils
  6. heat on low heat for a couple minutes

Enjoy the soup!
