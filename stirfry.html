Basic Single-Serve Potato and Egg Stirfry

Make this simple potato and egg stirfry!

    Ingredient List
  • 2-3 mini potatoes
  • 1 whole shallot or 1/2 onion
  • 2 small peppers
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp basil
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp cayenne
  • 1 tbsp virgin olive oil
    Steps to Cook
  1. chop and dice shallot/onion, peppers and potatoes
  2. scramble egg in bowl
  3. heat olive oil over medium heat in small pan
  4. put shallots/onions and potatoes in pan over medium heat
  5. add basil, oregano, and paprika
  6. heat for 5 - 10 minutes until shallots/onions are browned at edges
  7. remove shallots/onions and potatoes
  8. add peppers and cook for a couple minutes
  9. put scrambled eggs in pan
  10. add pepper and cayenne
  11. stir and cook on medium or low heat until the egg is cooked
  12. mix with potatoes and shallots/onions, then serve

That is how you make a potato and egg stirfry!
