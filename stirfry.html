Egg and Potato Stirfry

This is a great stirfry for breakfast

Ingredients List

  • 5 small potatoes
  • 1 shallot
  • 2 sweet peppers
  • 1 egg
  • Garlic powder
  • Cayenne
  • Basil

How to make a Potato and Egg Stirfry

  1. Chop and dice potatoes and shallots.
  2. Pour olive oil in the pan and heat on medium heat.
  3. Put potatoes and shallots in pan with garlic powder, cayenne, and basil and heat until the onions are cooked.
  4. Chop and dice sweet peppers, put in pan and cook until soft.
  5. Remove vegetables. Put egg in pan and cook sunny side up.
  6. Put egg on vegetables, mix together
