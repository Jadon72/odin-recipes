Simple Honey Peanutbutter Microwave Mug Cake

This microwave mug cake is a quick and easy desert to make when you are craving something sweet.

Ingredients List

  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp peanutbutter
  • 1 tbsp honey

How to make Simple Honey and Peanutbutter Microwave Mug Cake

  1. Combine egg, sugar and peanutbutter in a mug.
  2. Microwave for 1 minute.
  3. Drizzle honey over the cake.

The cake is finished!
