Creamy Egg Breakfast Burritos

This burrito recipe will make around 4-5 burritos. They are great to freeze and eat later.

Ingredients List

  • 4-5 Tortilla shells
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 whole onion
  • 1 jalapeno pepper
  • 2-3 sweet peppers
  • 2-3 mini potatoes
  • 1/2 cup cooked lentils
  • Extra firm tofu
  • Garlic powder
  • Turmeric
  • Paprika
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Burrito Filling Instructions

  1. Put olive oil in pan and heat over medium heat.
  2. Chop and dice onion and potatoes.
  3. Add onions and potatoes to heated pan, add garlic powder, turmeric, and paprika to taste. Continue heating until onions are clear and flexible, stirring occasionally.
  4. Chop sweet pepper and jalapenos, add into the pan for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Add lentils and tofu into the pan, heat until warm.
  6. Remove from pan into bowl.
  7. Add a small amount of olive oil for eggs, heat on medium low heat.
  8. Crack eggs and place into a bowl.
  9. Add cottage cheese to the eggs, and mix together until the egg is broken up.
  10. Heat egg-cottage cheese mixture in pan, stirring every 30 seconds until cooked (about 4-5 minutes).
  11. Remove from pan and thoroughly mix with the previously cooked vegetables.

How to fold your burritos

  1. Warm a tortilla on low heat in the pan or microwave for 5 seconds to make it flexible.
  2. Place 2-3 spoonfuls of fillings slightly off center of the middle of the tortilla.
  3. Fold the sides of the tortilla over the filling so that they almost touch, but do not quite meet.
  4. Fold the two other sides of the burrito like folding a present.
  5. Slowly roll one present-folded end of the burrito toward the other.
  6. You should now have a burrito!

How to reheat a frozen burrito

  • To reheat, place the burrito in a damp paper towel.
  • Microwave for 1 minute and 30 seconds, flip, microwave for another 1 minute and 30 seconds. Microwave time may very depending on microwave.
  • Optionally, you may crisp the burrito in an oven for 5-10 minutes at 350 degrees.
