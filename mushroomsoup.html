Mushroom and Cottagecheese Soup

This healthy mushroom and cottagecheese soup is packed with nutrience.

Ingredients List

  • Olive oil
  • 2 cup mushrooms
  • 4 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • Cottagecheese
  • Garlic powder
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 cup lentils
  • 1/2 cup tofu

How to make Mushroom Cottagecheese Soup

  1. Saute onions in garlic powder
  2. Add mushrooms into pan and cook until they start to brown, roughly 10 minutes.
  3. Add vegetable broth and bring to a simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Stir in lentils and tofu and heat for a minute until warm.
  5. Take off heat and stir cottage cheese in until well combined.
